🛒 Group Buying for Food & Provisions on Campus.

Campus Collective lets students in the same hostel or campus join group orders for food and provisions. The bigger the group, the lower the price — everyone saves when the threshold is hit.

Join a Group Buy Sign In

How Group Buying Works

  1. Browse Group Orders. See active group windows for food & provisions on your campus.
  2. Join Your Group. Students in your hostel or block join the same order window.
  3. Threshold Unlocks Price. When the minimum group size is met, everyone gets the lower group price.
  4. Campus Delivery. Order is confirmed and delivered to your hostel or campus hub.
